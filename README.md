My program will use pygame to create a platformer. The character will be able to dash, jump and more from left to right, 
jumping across platforms to reach the end and beat the game. There will also be powerups like speed up and double jump to help the player 
along their journey. However, the most important of these mechanics is the gravity mechanic to keep the player on the ground, the jump 
mechanic, and the mechanic that allows the player to move from left to right. The game will start on a title screen and when the player clicks 
start, it will take them into the game. From there the character will make their way around obstacles and if they die, they will respawn at 
their last checkpoint. Once they win, the player will get the option to quit the game or play it again.
